1. How do we collect information

 

We collect only the information necessary to realize the functions of the product in accordance with laws and regulations and the principles of legality, legitimacy, necessity and good faith. We generally collect and use your information for the following purposes:

 

1.1 Information required for user registration

 

If you only need the basic services available to you in a non-login state, you have the right to choose not to register as a user with us without providing any information. If you want to experience more of our services, you can choose to register as our user, you need to provide your registration information (personal email, mobile phone number, etc.) to complete the account registration and create a password.

 

1.2 Information required for providing services

 

When you install and use the relevant services of the APP, we will collect and use the information you proactively provide or generated by using the Services.

 

Respecting your privacy is our basic principle. We hereby explain to you in detail the content of APP's access to your information and provide an explanation. We will take various measures to protect your privacy and personal data.


  • Bluetooth or Wi-Fi:

When you use the APP to remotely manage your device, you need to communicate with the device connected via Wi-Fi. You can turn off Bluetooth or Wi-Fi at the end of use.


  • GPS:

When you use the Bluetooth function, turn on the GPS permission to connect to Bluetooth for communication. You can turn off the GPS positioning when you are finished using it.


  • Camera:

When you enter the car VIN code and activate VCI, we will call the camera for OCR recognition, and we promise not to capture and store images.

When you upload or change the avatar of your personal account, you can choose to use the camera to shoot directly and use it for personal image display. For the security of your information, we will only save the image you are using and promise to use it only for personal image display.

 

  • Image:

When you upload or change the avatar of your personal account, you can select a picture in the local album as your personal avatar. For the security of your information, we will only save the images you are using and promise to use them only for personal image display.

 

  • Video:

When you send us feedback, you have the option to send a video to our app. For the security of your information, we will only save the videos you send us and promise to only use them to improve user experience.

 

  • Document:

When you send us feedback, you can choose to send files to our application. For the security of your information, we will only save the PDF files you send us and promise to only use them to improve user experience.
